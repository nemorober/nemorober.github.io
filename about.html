My work starts from a sense of astonishment and awe for memories, feelings and thoughts,
life and death. Essential but elusive subjects for us human beings, who are ultimately faced
with the questions: why are we here and where are we going?

In my work I aim for maximum intensity and sensitivity. For me, colour is a language. Hence
the use of pastel. Pastel is pure pigment and therefore pure colour. Sometimes I combine
pastel with other materials, such as ink and watercolour and I use prints or old pictures to
integrate in my works.
